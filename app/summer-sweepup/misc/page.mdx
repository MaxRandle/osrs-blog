# Miscellaneous QoL improvements

## Managing Miscellania

Players generally don't like dailyscape. Allow players to earn up to 150% favour as a reward for completing the Fremennik Elite achievement diary, giving players up to 50 days of maximum efficiency before needing attention.

## Combat Achievements rewards

Combat Achievements rewards could really do with a boost. Some ideas for consideration:

- Double the task weight for boss slayer tasks for players who have completed the Elite tier of the combat achievements. This could be something that you'd toggle on/off so that EHPers can barrage smoke devils.
