The Hunllef is a pretty fun, intense, and well-designed fight but the enjoyability of the gauntlet is held back by the monotony of the preparation phase.

Introducing...

## The Fractal Gauntlet

After defeating the Corrupted gauntlet, players may attempt a new challenge. Players have **10 minutes** of preparation time after which they must defeat the Hunllef **as many times as they can** with the resources they were able to gather.

Each consecutive kill increases loot. Dying or exiting early does not forfeit your loot. This encourages players to limit test.

- Shifts the emphasis of the content away the preparation and onto PVM skill.
- Entirely re-used content, therefore low dev resource commitment.
