## The Heartseeker arbalest

The Heartseeker arbalest is a HUGE gothic crossbow that shoots two javelins per shot.

This weapon was created to hunt vampires and thus has the vampire bane attribute, gaining bonus damage and accuracy against Vyres, Venators, Leeches, Bloodvelds, bats, anything that drinks blood.

### Special attack

Uses 50% special attack - Deals extra damage and reduces the targets flat armour by 1.
