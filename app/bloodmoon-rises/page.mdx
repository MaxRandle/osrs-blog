## Intro

The Heartseeker arbalest is a HUGE gothic crossbow that shoots javelins.

This weapon was created to hunt vampires and thus has the vampire bane attribute, working on Vyres, Venators, Leeches, Bloodvelds, bats, anything that drinks blood.
