## Tonalztics of Ralos re-work

<Row>

<NoShrink>![Tonalztics of Ralos](/media/tonalztics-of-ralos.png)</NoShrink>

This item is extremely difficult to acquire, taking a similar time to obtain on rate as an Enhanced crystal weapon seed but from a much more difficult PVM activity.

</Row>

The Tonalztics is supposed to be the big ticket reward from one of the hardest pieces of content in the game, but it falls flat and is far too niche. It has potential but it needs to be reworked.

There&apos;s a space for a high dps one-handed 3-tick ranged weapon with better accuracy than blowpipe and worse accuracy than Bowfa. Filling a similar role to the Eye of Ayak including the defence draining spec.

It's also worth noting that if the Tonalztics were more widely used it would provide a great sink for Sunfire splinters.

**Suggestion:**

- **Increase attack speed from 6 tick to 3 tick**
- **Reduce ranged strength from 55 to 36**
- **Boost spec damage to compensate**

Here's a comparison of various setups against:

- Bloodveld (low defence target)
- Leviathan (medium defence target)
- Nex (high defence target)

**Conclusion:**

This isn't a Bowfa skip item, it's universally worse than either Bowfa or Blowpipe with mid-game gear.

But with end-game gear this item scales really effectively due to it hitting twice. It shines against medium defence targets and would be a new BiS at Leviathan.
