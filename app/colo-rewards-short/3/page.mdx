## Tonalztics of Ralos re-work

<Row justify="center">

<NoShrink>![Tonalztics of Ralos](/media/tonalztics-of-ralos.png)</NoShrink>

The big ticket reward from one of the hardest pieces of content in the game, taking similar time to obtain on rate as the Bowfa.

</Row>

The Tonalztics has potential, it's very cool but too niche, needs a re-work. Imagine this weapon filling a similar role to the Eye of Ayak.

**Suggestion: Re-work Tonalztics into a 3-tick dps weapon, keeping spec the same.**
