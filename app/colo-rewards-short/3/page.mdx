## Tonalztics of Ralos re-work

<Row justify="center">

<NoShrink>![Tonalztics of Ralos](/media/tonalztics-of-ralos.png)</NoShrink>

The big ticket reward from one of the hardest pieces of content in the game, taking similar time to obtain on rate as an Enhanced crystal weapon seed

</Row>

The Tonalztics has potential, it's very cool but too niche, needs a re-work. Imagine this weapon filling a similar role to the Eye of Ayak.

**Suggestion: Re-work Tonalztics into a high dps one-handed 3-tick ranged weapon.**

- Increase attack speed from 6 tick to 3 tick
- Reduce ranged strength from 55 to 36
- Keep same spec
