## New use for Echo crystals

Echo boots are universally worse than Avernic treads, this item needs a new thematic gear tie-in.

**Suggestion: Amulet of Echo fury**

<Row>
Combining the Echo crystal with an Amulet of fury creates the amulet of Echo fury with 10k charges.

<NoShrink>![Echo crystal](/media/echo-crystal.png)</NoShrink>

</Row>

<Row>
<NoShrink>![Echo fury](/media/echo-fury.png)</NoShrink>

<Col>

**passive effect:**

- Whenever you take damage you deal 2 damage back to the attacker.
- Whenever you take damage from an attack that you were praying correctly against, you will also heal 2 hitpoints and lose 1 prayer point.

</Col>

</Row>

This amulet serves as a situationally higher dps alternative to the Blood fury.
