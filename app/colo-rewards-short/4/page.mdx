## New uses for Echo crystals

Echo boots are universally worse than Avernic treads, this item needs a new thematic gear tie-in.

**Suggestion 1: Amulet of Echo fury**

<Row>
Combining an Echo crystal with an Amulet of fury creates the amulet of Echo fury with 6,000 charges, serving as a situational Blood fury alternative.

<NoShrink>![Echo crystal](/media/echo-crystal.png)</NoShrink>

</Row>

<Row>
<NoShrink><img className="w-16 mb-2 drop-shadow-black drop-shadow-sm" src={"/media/echo-fury.png"} alt="Echo fury" /></NoShrink>

<Col>

**Passive effect:**

- Whenever you take damage you deal 2 damage back to the attacker.
- Whenever you take damage from an attack that you were praying correctly against, you will also heal 2 hitpoints and lose 1 prayer point.

</Col>

</Row>
